Behind the scenes in a news broadcast studio session

As a journalism student, visiting behind the scenes of a news broadcast studio session was an eye-opening experience. I watched as producers coordinated every segment with precision, anchors prepared for live reports under intense studio lights, and camera operators worked seamlessly to capture each shot. The fast-paced environment highlighted the importance of timing, teamwork, and adaptability. Seeing how scripts, teleprompters, and live feeds came together in real-time deepened my appreciation for the technical and creative effort that goes into delivering the news accurately and professionally.

Visit to Uhuru Park

Visiting Uhuru Park was both inspiring and insightful. Nestled in the heart of Nairobi, the park was alive with activity—families relaxing, street performers entertaining crowds, and vendors offering colorful snacks. I captured moments of everyday life through my lens: children chasing bubbles, elders engaged in animated conversation, and young couples enjoying quiet moments by the fountain. The vibrant atmosphere and diverse interactions made it a perfect spot to observe and document human stories, reminding me of the power of journalism in capturing real, unfiltered slices of life.

Going out for a walk

As a journalism student, going out for a walk turned into an opportunity to observe and capture the subtle stories unfolding around me. With my notebook and camera in hand, I documented everyday moments—a street vendor setting up his stall, a child skipping beside her mother, and an elderly man quietly feeding birds on a park bench. The rhythm of the city came alive through these small, unscripted scenes. Each step offered a new perspective, reminding me that compelling stories often exist in the simplest of moments, waiting to be noticed and told.

Invitation to a Corporate Event

Attending a corporate event offered a unique glimpse into the professional world of business communication and networking. I observed keynote speakers delivering powerful messages, guests engaging in thoughtful conversations, and teams celebrating shared achievements. Moments I captured included the handshake between two CEOs, a panelist passionately addressing the audience, and candid exchanges over coffee during breaks. The polished setting, strategic branding, and formal atmosphere contrasted sharply with the everyday stories I was used to, giving me a broader perspective on how stories are told and perceived in the corporate space.

Receiving flowers from a loved one

As a journalism student, receiving a flower gift from a loved one was a tender moment that reminded me of the human side behind every story. I captured the gentle details—the soft petals wrapped in simple paper, the handwritten note tucked inside, and the warmth in their eyes as they handed it to me. In a world often focused on headlines and breaking news, this quiet gesture spoke volumes about love, connection, and the small, beautiful moments that deserve to be remembered and shared.
